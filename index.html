About Us

Crafting beauty with passion and precision

Our Story

Founded in 2023, KHULI Hair & Nails Salon began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where beauty meets artistry. Our founder, Khuliso Mahafha, envisioned a space where clients could experience luxurious hair and nail services in an environment that feels both indulgent and welcoming.

Today, our team of certified stylists and nail artists continue this tradition, combining technical expertise with creative flair to deliver exceptional results for every client.

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality products and staying at the forefront of beauty trends, ensuring your look is both stunning and timeless.
